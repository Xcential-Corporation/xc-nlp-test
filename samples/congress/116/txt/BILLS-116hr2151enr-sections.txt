Section 1. Senior Chief Petty Officer Shannon M. Kent Post Office.(a) Designation.—The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 7722 South Main Street in Pine Plains, New York, shall be known and designated as the “Senior Chief Petty Officer Shannon M. Kent Post Office”.(b) References.—Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the “Senior Chief Petty Officer Shannon M. Kent Post Office”.
