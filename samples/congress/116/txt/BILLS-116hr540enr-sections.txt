Section 1. Louise and Bob Slaughter Post Office.(a) Designation.—The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 770 Ayrault Road in Fairport, New York, shall be known and designated as the “Louise and Bob Slaughter Post Office”.(b) References.—Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the “Louise and Bob Slaughter Post Office”.
