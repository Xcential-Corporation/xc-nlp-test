Section 1. Jerry C. Washburn Post Office Building.(a) Designation.—The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 877 East 1200 South in Orem, Utah, shall be known and designated as the “Jerry C. Washburn Post Office Building”.(b) References.—Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the “Jerry C. Washburn Post Office Building”.
