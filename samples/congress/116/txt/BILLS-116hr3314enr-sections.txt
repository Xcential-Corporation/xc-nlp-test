Section 1. Lake Havasu City Combat Veterans Memorial Post Office Building.(a) Designation.—The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1750 McCulloch Boulevard North in Lake Havasu City, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the “Lake Havasu City Combat Veterans Memorial Post Office Building”.(b) References.—Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the “Lake Havasu City Combat Veterans Memorial Post Office Building”.
